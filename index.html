MANAGING YOUR STUDIES

Time Management And It's Benefits

Undertaking a course at university is a big commitment, both in time and energy. Dedication needs to be given towards classes, as well as studying the course materials during the semester. This affects the amount of time given to personal aspects of life such as socializing and hobbies. Learning to balance all these factors is an important life skill and to aid this is the use of effective time management.

Time is precious, yet the illusion exists that there is not enough time available. With effective time management however, this illusion disappears and reveals not only the abundant amount of time available, but also the extensive list of benefits it can achieve.

The Benefits

Here are just some examples of the benefits of effective time management.

  • Complete Assessments on time – Dealing with assessments close to the due date can lead to a loss of focus and decrease the quality of work completed. By allocating time to the assessment when it is first issued, the task will be complete by the due date and most of the time beforehand leading to extra time towards personal activities.
  • Improve Motivation – Keeping a schedule helps the mind and body focus. Knowing what task is to be worked on and what activities lies ahead cuts out unnecessary time deciding on the task to undertake. This means that all tasks related to studying can be completed earlier than expected and open spare time for personal activities. Handing assessments in early is also gives motivation towards completing the next assignment and gaining more personal time, instead of generating stress about upcoming assessments and the illusion of falling behind with workload.
  • Gradual Grade Improvement – As assessments are being undertaken following a schedule, more care and focus is given towards them as the stress of the due date is not impeding on the mind. This means that the work produced will be of a higher quality than when it is completed while under stress. It also means there is time to review the work completed, remove and small mistakes, or improve upon work that is decided as unsatisfactory. The quality of the work and effort put in will reflect on the grades received as they increase, which will give motivation towards excelling in the next assessment, and the course overall.
  • Improve Reliability – Benefits of effective time management not only impact studying, but also personal image as well. Meeting deadlines, arriving to appointments on time, and being punctual are just some of these benefits. This will also be noticed by colleagues, family and peers as time passes, improving image and reliability shown towards others.

By keeping a managed schedule of all activities and allocating time towards each effectively, it can lead help balance life activities while also achieving desired goals. By learning how to break down time effectively as shown in the next page, it will help achieve the many benefits of effective time management.
